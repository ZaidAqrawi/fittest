Install App

How to Install

  1. 1 Click the "Install App" button above.
  2. 2 Wait for the app icon to appear on your home screen. The download may take a few moments depending on your internet connection.
  3. 3 Before you can open the app, you need to trust the developer profile. Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management (or Device Management on older iOS versions).
  4. 4 Tap on your developer account name, then tap "Trust [Developer Name]" and confirm.
  5. 5 Return to your home screen and tap the app icon to launch it.

App Won't Open? Troubleshooting

  1. 1. No Profile Showing in Settings? This means your device UDID is NOT registered in the provisioning profile. You need to:
    • Get your iPad's UDID: Settings → General → About → Tap on "Identifier" to copy it
    • Add it to your Apple Developer account at developer.apple.com
    • Update the provisioning profile to include your device
    • Rebuild and export a new IPA with the updated profile
  2. 2. Trust the Developer Profile: If a profile appears, go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management (or Device Management). Find your developer profile and tap "Trust [Developer Name]" twice to confirm.
  3. 3. Reinstall: Delete the app, then reinstall it from this page. Make sure to trust the profile before opening.
  4. 4. Check Console Logs: Connect your iPad to a Mac and check Console.app for crash logs if the app closes immediately.

Important Notes

  • • You must open this page in Safari on your iOS device (not Chrome or Firefox)
  • • Your device must be registered in the developer's provisioning profile
  • • The app will only work on devices that have been added to the Ad-Hoc distribution profile
