Install App

Installation Instructions:

  1. Tap the "Install App" button above
  2. If prompted, tap "Install" in the popup
  3. Go to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management
  4. Trust the developer certificate
  5. Return to your home screen and open the app
Note: This app requires iOS and must be installed on an iPhone or iPad. Make sure you trust the developer certificate in Settings after installation.
